Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston Reunite at 2020 SAG Awards

  • At the 26th annual Screen Actors Guild Awards, Jennifer Aniston won the award for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Drama Series for her work on The Morning Show.
  • Ex-husband Brad Pitt, who earlier accepted the award for Best Male Actor in a Supporting Role, made sure to catch the speech even though he wasn't in the room.
  • The two also ran into each other backstage.

The last time Jennifer Aniston won a Screen Actors Guild award, she was starring on Friends and had yet to date her future ex-husband Brad Pitt. Now, over 20 years and a few relationships later, both Aniston and Pitt have become winners again at the 26th annual SAG Awards–and they're proving once and for all that there's no bad blood between them.

While making her acceptance speech for the Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Drama Series for her role on The Morning Show, Brad Pitt was nowhere to be seen. But, in a behind-the-scenes video captured by E!, we find that Pitt, who won the award for Best Male Actor in a Supporting Role just moments before, was watching all along backstage.

“Boy did we get to dive deep into our own experiences and our own history and really be able to breathe life into these extraordinary characters,” Aniston said while she was accepting her award. “Who knew that emotional breakdowns felt that good? It was literally about seven months of therapy that covered 20 years of work. So thank you for watching that.”

Pitt and Aniston also bumped into each other while backstage, where they exchanged big smiles with each other. He was even snapped grabbing onto Aniston's wrist, which set the Internet into a frenzy.
