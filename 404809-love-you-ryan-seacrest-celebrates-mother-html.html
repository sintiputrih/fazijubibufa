'Love You': Ryan Seacrest Celebrates Mother Connie's Birthday With a Throwback Photo

Ryan Seacrest marks a family occasion with a throwback photo as the TV host turned to social media to celebrate his mother's birthday.

A young Ryan Seacrest is captured grinning, standing beside his mother in her younger years. His mother is seen beaming as well, holding a large birthday cake adorned with several candles.

Ryan also tags his sister Meredith, who stands on the right side of their mother. Through this, Ryan unveils Meredith's social media page, which remains private and solely includes a link to the Ryan Seacrest Charity Foundation.

Ryan wrote that he couldn't wait to celebrate his mom's special day with dinner later and added in the caption, "Love you." A few hours later, Seacrest shared another post featuring two images of his parents.

While he and his father, Gary, are stylishly dressed in perfectly tailored suits, his mother, Connie, dazzles in a floral off-shoulder dress. In this post, Ryan Seacrest reveals that the family festivities have kicked off and mentions they will continue celebrating throughout the week.

Seacrest is never shy to show his love and pride for his parents, especially when it comes to their long-lasting marriage. At the beginning of 2023, the couple celebrated their 53rd wedding anniversary. Ryan says that the secret of their long-lasting relationship is communication. Also, they actually celebrate the years spent together.

When it was their 50th anniversary of marriage, Ryan shared a collage of their "then-now" photos. While the first one featured his parents on their wedding day, the second one shows them two decades later somewhere on the seafront.

Last year, Gary and Connie visited the "Live with Kelly and Ryan" set in support of their son. Ryan had recently said farewell to the show he hosted with Kelly Ripa. Kelly's husband, Mark Consuelos has taken over from the former host and celebrated his first day on April 17.
