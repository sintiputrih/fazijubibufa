Kandy Johnson Isley - Age, Family, Bio

Wife of legendary singer Ron Isley, who was the lead singer and founder of the Isley Brothers. She is a former singer and songwriter. 

Before Fame

Before becoming a backup singer, she was a part of the R&B group the Johnson Sisters with her sister Kim. They were later signed to the Isley Brothers' management company in 2001.

Trivia

She and her sister Kim were backup singers for decades, as they have performed with artists such as Michael Jackson, Aretha Franklin, Christina Aguilera and Sting.

Family Life

In 2005, she married her husband in Beverly Hills, California. Two years later, they welcomed their only child, Ron Isley Jr. 

Associated With

She shares a February 24 birthday with Rachel Levin.
