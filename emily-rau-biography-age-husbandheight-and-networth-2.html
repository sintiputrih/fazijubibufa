Emily Rau Biography

Emily Rau is an Emmy award-winning journalist based in St. Louis, Missouri. She is a freelance journalist at ABC News. The Journalist was born in Washington, Missouri to parents Andrea and Michael Rau. In 2007, She was named Queen of Country Fair and Washington Town.

Emily Rau Career

Emily spent five years at KMOV, the CBS affiliate in St. Louis, Missouri. Her work ethic and team spirit propelled her from one-man-band bureau reporter to morning anchor in less than three years. Emily’s ability to connect with the audience contributed to unprecedented rating growth for the three-hour newscast.
A two-time regional Emmy winner, Emily also earned the Best Anchor award from the Missouri Broadcasters Association. Gazelle STL magazine recently named her one of the ‘Top 50 Women in St. Louis.’ Emily is a Missouri native and fourth-generation graduate of the University of Missouri- Columbia.
