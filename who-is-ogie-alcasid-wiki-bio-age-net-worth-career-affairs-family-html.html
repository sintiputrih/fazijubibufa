Who is Ogie Alcasid? Wiki, Bio, Age, Net Worth, Career, Affairs,Family

Ogie Alcasid is a prominent figure in the Filipino entertainment industry, wearing multiple hats as a musician, actor, comedian, parodist, and television host. With a rich and diverse career spanning various artistic avenues, he has left an indelible mark in the world of entertainment.

Who is Ogie Alcasid?

  • Ogie Alcasid’s journey began with his membership in the 1984 batch of the all-male singing ensemble Kundirana, following in the footsteps of Gary Valenciano.
  • His debut album was released in 1989, setting the stage for his multifaceted career in the entertainment industry.
  • Ogie Alcasid’s directorial debut came in 1990 with “Feel Na Feel,” released by Regal Entertainment. His debut feature for VIVA Films, “Pitong Gamol,” followed in 1991.

A Versatile Career

  • Ogie Alcasid embraced the world of television in 1992 by co-hosting the comedy program “Small Brothers” on ABS-CBN.
  • He became a familiar face on various comedic shows, including ABC’s “Tropang Trumpo” (1994–1995), GMA Network’s “Bubble Gang” (1995–2013), and QTV’s “Ay, Robot!” (2005–2007), among others.
  • Ogie’s career expanded to hosting game shows, starting with ABC-5’s “Family Feud” in the Philippines in 200He also hosted GMA’s “Celebrity Duets: Philippine Edition.”
  • A major breakthrough came with his debut drama series, “Hanggang Kailan,” where he performed the theme song with Aiza Seguerra. This marked his transition from ABS-CBN to GMA Network in 1995.

Marriage and Relationships

  • Prior to his marriage to Regine Velasquez, Ogie was married to Michelle Van Eimeren from 1996 to 2010.

Family and Siblings
