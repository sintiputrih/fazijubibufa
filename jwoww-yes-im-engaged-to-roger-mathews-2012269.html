JWoww: Yes, I'm Engaged to Roger Mathews!

JWoww is getting ready to fist-pump her way down the aisle!

MTV's Jersey Shore and Snooki & JWoww star (real name: Jenni Farley) accepted boyfriend Roger Mathews' proposal – and a dazzling pink and white engagement ring.

Together for two-and-a-half years, JWoww, 26, and Mathews, 37, got engaged during a high-flying skydiving session when Mathews presented his girlfriend with a ring that totaled 5 carats and features 2.5 carats in diamonds surrounding the center stone. (WetPaint broke news of the engagement last week.)

"Thank you everyone on the engagement wishes," the reality star tweeted Wednesday.
