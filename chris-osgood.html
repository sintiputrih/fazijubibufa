Who is Chris Osgood:

Chris Osgood is a famous Hockey Player. He was born on November 26, 1972 and his birthplace is Peace River, Canada. Chris is also well known as, NHL goaltender who helped lead the Detroit Red Wings to a nine-game playoff winning streak on the way to a 2008 Stanley Cup Championship.

Chris is originated from Canada. After he retired, the Red Wings brought in Jimmy Howard to fill in as net-minder.
