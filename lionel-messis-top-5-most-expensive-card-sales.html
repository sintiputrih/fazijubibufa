Lionel Messi's Top 5 Most Expensive Card Sales

A legendary football player, Lionel Messi, has a value for his trading card that remains one of the most extravagant figures in paraphernalia market. Here is an insight into the top 5 most expensive card sales of more than 5 figure amounts and where to find such data.


1. 2004-05 Panini Megacracks #71

Sale Price: $336,000
One of the most iconic and in-demand Lionel Messi cards, 2004-05 Panini Megacracks #71. It is Messi's actual true rookie card and as a result, it fetches quite a price point due to its scarcity. Only 19 PSA gave this card a perfect grade, with its top bid hitting $336,000. This card, then - a permanent reminder of Messi's legacy - is one that will continue to be revered by soccer collectors as the years wane on.


2. 2014 Panini Prizm World Cup Gold Prizm /50

Sale Price: $500,000+
The 2014 Panini Prizm World Cup Gold Prizm #12 would prove to be Messi's very first Panini Prizm card. With just 10 of these cards available and only 2 PSA-graded Zhu Rong, Value jump up to $500k in the year 2022. What makes this card significant is that it depicts Messi in the lead-up to the FIFA World Cup 2014 Finals thus making for a key piece of any collectible collection.


3. 2022 Panini Prizm World Cup Qatar #7 - Black Prizm 1/1

Sale Price: $159,584
Lionel Messi scooped his first-ever World Cup Championship in 2022, cementing a legacy as the GOAT of football. This 1/1 Black Prizm parallel sold at Goldin Auctions for $159,584. It only makes this incredibly rare card even more iconic and valuable in Messi's history.


4. Messi 2004 Panini Sports Mega Cracks Rookie Card #71

Sale Price: $336,000
The 2004-05 Panini Sports Mega Cracks card of Lionel Messi, is likely considered his rookie card and one of the top prizes in the sports memorabilia market. A PSA 10-graded Messi rookie card is extremely rare mainly due to the limited number of these cards available and also because this man has shown time and again why he's called one of the greatest players ever. This $336k card finding a home is simply proof of Messi's legacy on the world stage in sporting collectibles.


5. 2014 Panini Prizm Lionel Messi World Cup Green Crystal Prizm #12

Sale Price: $61,200
Numbered /25, This looks like Lionel Messi's road to the 2014 FIFA World Cup Final and is Considered Cylinder Patch Autograp (PA-LM), a very rare card for collectors. The 2014 Panini Prizm Lionel Messi World Cup Green Crystal Prizm, of which only two in the world exist, sold for an impressive $61,200 at Goldin Auctions--more proof that the market strength surrounding Messi is still undeniable.
