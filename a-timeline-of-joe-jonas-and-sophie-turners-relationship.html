A Timeline Of Joe Jonas And Sophie Turner's Relationship

When it comes to celebrity couples, Game of Thrones star Sophie Turner and Jonas Brothers member Joe Jonas are definitely amongst the most famous ones. The two have been in a relationship for almost 5 years now and they have certainly come along way since their beginnings. One reason why fans absolutely love Sophie and Joe is that the couple always seemed very authentic, real, and relatable!

Related: A Timeline Of Gigi Hadid And Zayn Malik's Relationship

Today’s list to take a look at their relationship timeline — from Joe sliding into Sophie Turner’s Instagram DMs to them welcoming their daughter Willow into the world.

10 In 2016 Joe Jonas Slid Into Sophie Turner's DMs On Instagram

We are kicking the list off all the way back in 2016 when the couple got together. Prior to dating each other, the two have been linked to quite a few other famous celebs — until Joe Jonas made the first move by sliding into Sophie Turner’s Instagram DM’s. According to the Game of Thrones star, the two already had quite a few mutual friends and it was only a matter of time before they met in real life.

9 And They Went On Their First Date To A Local Bar In Camden, UK

In an interview with Elle Magazine, Sophie Turner opened up about her first date with Joe Jonas which was at a local bar in Camden, UK. To the date, Sophie brought plenty of her male friends because she thought that Joe might be a catfish. To her surprise, the two ended up spending the rest of the night chatting away like they knew each other for years — and the rest is pretty much history!

8 After Which The Couple Officially Started Dating

Soon after their first date, things got a bit more serious between the two stars — and the public quickly caught on.

Related: Inside Amy Adams And Christian Bale's Relationship

Of course, keeping a relationship secret is pretty much impossible in the world of the rich and famous as paparazzi are really good at spotting celebrity couples. Since 2016 Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner have been dating — and they often graced red carpets together!

7 And By October 2017 The Famous Couple Got Engaged

In October 2017 — after approximately a year of dating — the couple decided to get engaged and they certainly seemed like they couldn't be happier. Whether it was paparazzi photos or fancy red carpet pics — by then everyone knew that Joe Jonas and SophieTurner are absolutely head-over-heels in love with each other!

6 That Year The Couple Decided To Adopt A Puppy Together

Those who follow Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner on Instagram already know that the couple has an adorable puppy which they decided to adopt together in 2017. In the photo above, the two can be seen on a walk with their beloved family member — but as fans know, since then their family expanded!

5 Sophie Turner And Joe Jonas Got Married In Las Vegas On May 1, 2019

On May 1, 2019, Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas got married by an Elvis impersonator in the presence of their closest friends and family at a ceremony in Las Vegas. Yes, originally the two didn’t go for a big and glamorous wedding but rather for a very fun and relaxed one. Joe and Sophie are definitely one of the coolest couples in the industry which is why they totally pulled off having such a funky wedding.

4 And They Repeated Their Marriage Vows On June 29, 2019, In France

While the two did officially get married in May 2019 — they decided to repeat their marriage vows on June 29, 2019, at a stunning ceremony at the Château de Tourreau in the south of France.

This wedding ceremony included plenty of white flowers, glass candles, and gorgeously dressed guests — and it was definitely a proper fairytale wedding!

3 In 2019 Sophie Starred In Jonas Brothers' Music Video For "Sucker"

The Jonas Brothers have certainly done quite a lot since their Disney Channel days and in 2019 they decided to reunite after a long hiatus. In the music video for "Sucker" — their first single released after their reunion — Joe, Nick, and Kevin decided to have their wives star beside them. Above is a still from the music video in which Joe and Sophie can be seen!

2 And A Year Later She Also Starred In Ther Music Video For "What A Man Gotta Do"

Another Jonas Brothers music video that Sophie Turner as well as Priyanka Chopra and Danielle Jonas appeared in is the one for their 2020 hit "What A Man Gotta Do". As seen from the still above — for the appearance Sophie rocked a black wig and frankly, it certainly suited the Game of Thrones star very well!

1 And In July 2020 Sophie Gave Birth To Their Daughter

Wrapping the list up is the fact that in July 2020 — in the middle of a global pandemic — Sophie Turner (who was certainly pampered a lot by her husband Joe during her pregnancy) gave birth to their daughter. The couple decided to name their daughter Willa and while fans are yet to see their little bundle of joy — there is no doubt that Sophie and Joe will be amazing parents!
