Professional Life

During middle school, Wi Ha-joon joined the dance club and dreamed of becoming an entertainer after joining the dance club.

In his senior year, the actor moved to Seoul and started attending an acting academy with the hope of becoming an actor.

Moreover, the actor considered Song Kang Ho and Kim Woo Bin as his role models. During his first year in university, the actor was enlisted in the Korean Air Force as a military police.

In 2012, the actor made his acting debut with a short South Korean film titled “Peace in Them”. After that, the actor played the role of Young Woo-gon in the South Korean movie “Coin Locker Girl” in 2015.

In the same year, the actor also portrayed the role of Cha Myung-ho in another South Korean action comedy film “Bad Guys Always Die”.

Wi Ha-joon played the role of Jung-tae in the South Korean film “Eclipse” in 2016.

In the same year, the actor made his television debut as Ha-joon in the South Korean television series “Goodbye Mr. Black” by MBC Network.

After that, the actor played the role of Korean youth in prison in the film “Anarchist from Colony” and the role of Young Jung-hyuk in the film “The Chase” in 2017.

Moreover, the actor appeared as Ryu Jae-shin in the television series “My Golden Life” by KBS2 Network.

In 2018, the actor played his popular role of Ha-joon in the South Korean horror film “Gonjiam” Haunted Asylum”.

The actor also appeared as Yoon Seung-ho in “Something in the Rain”, as a guest in “Island Trio”, as Im Si-ho in “Matrimonial Chaos”, and as Lee Ha-Min in “With Coffee” in the same year.

2019 – Present

In 2019, he appeared as Jung Woo-jun in the film “Girls Cop”. The actor also appeared as Ji Seo-joon in the television series “Romance Is a Bonus Book” in the same year.

The actor appeared in two television series in 2020 as Oh Yoo-min in “Soul Mechanic” and as Ye Ji-hoon in “18 Again”.

In 2021, the actor appeared as Do-shik in the film “Midnight” and as Jeong Do-hyeon in the film “Shark: The Beginning”.

The actor also appeared as Hwang Joon-ho in the Netflix series “Squid Game” and as K in the television series “Bad and Crazy”.

Achievements

Wi Ha-joon received a nomination for Best New Actor at the 11th Korea Drama Awards for his performance in Something in the Rain in 2018.

The actor also received a nomination for Best New Actor at the 55th Grand Bell Awards, and 39th Blue Dragon Film Awards in the same year for his performance in the film “Gonjiam: Haunted Asylum”.

In 2019, he received a nomination for Best New Actor at 24th Chunsa Film Art Awards for his role in the film “Gonjiam: Haunted Asylum”.

The actor also received a nomination for Best New Actor (Television) at the 55th Baeksang Arts Awards for his performance in Romance Is a Bonus Book in the same year.

He has garnered a significant amount of money from his profession as an actor, model, and television actor.
