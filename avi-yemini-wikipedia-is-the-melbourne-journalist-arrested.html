Avi Yemini Wikipedia: Is The Melbourne Journalist Arrested?

Avi Yemini has not secured a Wikipedia page at the moment but he is not an unknown name in his country. Here’s what you’re missing from him! 

Avi Yemini is an Australian journalist and political activist. He is a far-right activist and is also a social media personality.

Having his career and interest rooted in many fields, he has become one of the well-known media personalities in Australia.

As of today, he has once again become the center of media attention when his arrest on Australia Day has sparked a lawsuit.

Avi Yemini Wikipedia And Age Explored

Avi Yemini’s current age is 35 years old as of 2021. He was born on October 17, 1985, in Melbourne.

Though he was born and raised in Australia, he is of mixed heritage.

He was born to a Russian-Polish father and a Yemeni mother. His family is Jewish and he was disowned by his family during his teen years for rejecting his religious restrictions.

Moreover, he also holds Israeli citizenship when he had moved to Israel as an adult.

He became a sharpshooter in the Israeli Defence Force (IDF) serving with the Golani Brigade from 2005 to 2008. he spent most of his active duty in the Gaza Strip.

After he had returned to Australia in 2008, he opened his own gym and also ventured into political journalism.

Over the course of time, he has been into many legal troubles with the police regarding his activism in the public.

Is He Arrested? What Happened To the Journalist?

Avi Yemini has been arrested many times in the past by the Melbourne Police Department regarding his journalism.

As of 2021, he is fighting a lawsuit against the police for his unlawful arrest on Australia Day.

According to Rebel News, this unlawful arrest and the continuous tussle between the two parties have brought Avi to fight them in court.

The next court listing date is set on 8 December 2021, where the court is likely to set the trial date. But right now, Avi and Rebel News are raising about $169,868 to ensure the trial.

Follow Avi Yemini On Instagram

Avi Yemini has an active presence on Instagram as well as on other social media platforms.

He is currently followed by more than 96k followers on Instagram while he has a dominating fan following on Twitter at nearly 170k followers.

Given his media journalism, it is for sure that he is followed by this many Australians.
