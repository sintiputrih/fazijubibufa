Hailey Bieber Channels Vixen Energy in Leather Trench Coat and Itty-Bitty Short Shorts

Hailey Bieber just won't stop wearing all-leather outfits—not that we're complaining.

The Rhode founder showed off her latest outfit via her Instagram Story last night, pulling the Matrix-esque look from Ferragamo's pre-fall 2023 collection. The outfit included a shiny leather trench coat in an inky-black color, as well as matching hot shorts cinched by a belt with a circular silver buckle. She kept things simple with the rest of the ensemble, opting for a classic plain white T-shirt, narrow rectangular sunglasses, and black pumps.

"Sour," she wrote over the mirror selfie.

Instagram

Instagram

Late last month, the model was spotted in more statement-making leather pieces.

While in New York City, she donned another all-leather uniform, this time wearing an oversized bomber with a tiny miniskirt. She layered the look with a slouchy black sweater and sheer tights, then accessorized with rectangular shades with tortoiseshell frames and a tiny patent-leather handbag by Saint Laurent.

Gotham//Getty Images

She wore a similar silhouette during a date night with husband Justin Bieber that same week. Paparazzi photographed Hailey looking edgy in an all-black outfit composed of an oversized leather blazer layered over yet another leather biker jacket, plus a gray sweater with a white collar peeking underneath. She ditched pants and, instead, opted for a tiny black miniskirt.

Hailey talked about about her fashion philosophy for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story.

"I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I'm just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
