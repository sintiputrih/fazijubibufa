See Jennifer Lopez's Patterned Yoga Pants and Green Bling Cup

Jennifer Lopez has made a stylish case for the yoga pant.

Yesterday, the pop star and Marry Me actress was spotted returning to the same Los Angeles studio that she visited Thursday. Instead of donning a pair of skintight leggings, the star went with an eye-catching pair of black and white geometric-pattern pants that flared at her calves. She paired the fun athleisure wear with a gray cropped sweatshirt, worn over strappy black sports bra.

The "Waiting for Tonight" singer finished the look with black and white sneakers and a pair of silver-rimmed sunglasses, wearing her hair in a top-knot bun. Instead of a gym bag, she carried one of her trusty bling cups, a green ombré cup that matched her bedazzled phone case.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

On Thursday, the former In Living Color dancer wore a sleeker workout look, comprised of a long-sleeved Kith Sport crop top featuring a faux turtleneck, black leggings in a metallic crocodile-skin print, and Nike sneakers. She also carried a rare white-and-gold Hermès Birkin bag as her gym bag.

She wore her hair in a sleek ponytail and accessorized the look with chunky silver earrings and black aviator sunglasses.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Lopez has carried dozens of custom Swarovski-encrusted cups throughout the years, and the star has admitted that she doesn't know how many she owns. The cups are crafted by "celebrity blinger" Teneka Moore of TaylorMade Bling Embellishments, who's worked with the star since 2017.

"I couldn't have asked for a better blessing. It's been so crazy," Moore told Entertainment Tonight about working with the singer. "Ever since then, she orders a cup for every event, every promotion that she does. Any movie, anything that she does, she commemorates it with a cup. It's awesome."
