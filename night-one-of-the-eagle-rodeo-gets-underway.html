Night One of the Eagle Rodeo gets underway

Eagle, ID (CBS2) — The 22nd annual Eagle Rodeo kicked off on Wednesday with retro night.

Fans and contestants were encouraged to break out those shirts and dresses from the '80s and '90s.

Thursday night is military appreciation night, Friday is tough enough to wear pink night, and Saturday is finals night.

The rodeo goes through Saturday with the gates opening at 5 pm. The junior rodeo gets underway at 6 pm followed by the main rodeo at 7:30 pm.

Tickets are still available online at https://www.eaglerodeo.com/schedule-tickets/, at the Star, State street (Garden City), and Chinden Human Bean locations. As well as the Glenwood, Overland, and Meridian D&B Supply locations.
