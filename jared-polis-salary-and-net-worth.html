Jared Polis salary and net worth

Jared Schutz Polis (born May 12, 1975) is an American politician, entrepreneur, businessman, and philanthropist who has served as Colorado’s 43rd governor since 2019.

Polis served one term on the Colorado State Board of Education from 2001 to 2007, and five terms as Colorado’s 2nd congressional district’s representative from 2009 to 2019.

He was the only Democratic member of the Liberty Caucus and a libertarian Democrat. He was elected governor of Colorado in 2018 and easily re-elected in 2022.
