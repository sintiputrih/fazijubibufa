School Bus, Car Collide On Belt Parkway

After a car and a school bus were involved in a collision on the Belt Parkway at Pennsylvania Avenue early on Tuesday morning, all lanes heading in the eastern direction were shut down.

In addition, emergency vehicles obstructed lanes on the westbound side, which contributed to the delays in traffic.

The vehicle was crushed below the school bus, and the front two wheels of the bus were resting on top of the railing in the middle.

It occurred immediately after 9 o’clock in the morning close to Exit 13.
