Oliver Peck is a well-known tattoo artist from the United States. He is well-known as the co-owner of Elm Street Tattoo in Texas. In addition, Oliver is the owner of True Tattoo in Los Angeles. Aside from that, he serves as a judge on Ink Master.

Early Life and Childhood

Oliver was born on July 29, 1971, in Dallas, Texas, USA, and is currently 51 years old. He is Diane Lucas’s son. He had a strong interest in drawing and art since he was a child.

Likewise, He used to be a skater when he was younger. He used to be a fantastic skater in school. His nationality is American, and he is of white Caucasian descent.

Aside from that, there is no further detail available about his father, siblings, or early childhood life. Considering his success, we can believe that he was raised by his parents in his hometown.

In terms of education, he attended South West High School in his hometown. However, he was uninterested in the study.

In addition, he started to use drugs and was arrested. At the age of 17, he noticed his passion for tattooing and started to focus on it.

Professional Life

Oliver started his tattooing career when he was just 17 years old. However, he began his professional career after Stell saw his talent in tattoos.

Stell even took him to Dallas and placed him in his tattoo shop, Pair-O-Dice Tattoo. Stell was training him professionally at the time.

Furthermore, he is one of the world’s best tattoo artists and broke his ex-wife Kat Von D’s Guinness World Record by inking 415 tattoos in 24 hours.

Peck has been well-known for his traditional tattoo look over the years. Peck focused heavily on classic American traditional style and has rarely drifted from it to this day.

He was a strong believer in keeping it simple, strong, bold, and clean. He captured the American spirit and infused it with a modern spirit with his tattoos for the perfect execution.

In addition, he is a judge on Spike TV’s Ink Master, alongside Dave Navarro and Chris Nunez.
