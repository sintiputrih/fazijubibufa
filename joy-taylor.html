Joy Taylor – Professional Life and Career

While attending Barry University, Joy Taylor began her career as a radio show host. She also worked as a manager for WBRY 1640 AM, the University’s radio station. She was a part of CBSSports.com’s Thursday Night Live and Fantasy Football Today before joining Fox Sports in 2016. Taylor previously worked at 790 AM The Ticket, where she worked as an executive producer before becoming the co-host of ‘The Ticket Morning Show,’ a morning sports radio show on 790 AM The Ticket.

On March 17, 2016, the American beauty made her debut television appearance, as a guest news reporter on Fox Sports 1’s ‘The Herd with Colin Cowherd’ radio talk show. Soon after, she joined Fox Sports 1’s ‘Skip and Shannon Undisputed’ sports debate show as a moderator, with commentators Skip Bayless and former CBS Sports analyst and former NFL tight end, Shannon Sharpe.

She also has a Facebook Live show called The Hang and a podcast called “Maybe I’m Crazy.” In 2018, Fox announced that Taylor would transition from Undisputed to The Herd with Colin Cowherd, which would stream on FS1 and Fox Sports Radio beginning June 18. Taylor was named as the anchor of her own Saturday show, The Joy Taylor Show, on Fox Sports Radio on September 9, 2021.
