WWE Legends Son Becomes The Newest Member Of The Bullet Club

The Bullet Club is regarded as one of the greatest groups in Japanese history and even in the entire world of professional wrestling. Throughout the faction’s history in wrestling, it has seen various incarnations under the leadership of talented leaders, who are big names in the industry. Most recently, Jay White was the man in charge of Bullet Club but now has been replaced by a new member, David Finlay.

David Finlay brings immense in-ring talent, skills and a legacy to be carried forward in the industry. He is the son of the WWE Legend, Fit Finlay, who has been a pioneer in the business in his own right.

On the other hand, after losing his final match with the organization to Eddie Kingston at Battle in the Valley, Jay White left New Japan Pro-Wrestling. He was attacked by David Finlay, someone he has known for years, after the match.

David Finlay became the newest Bullet Club member during the ongoing New Japan Cup 2023. He was accompanied by Gedo, who served as Jay White’s former manager and stablemate.

David Finlay has spent most of his New Japan tenure as a babyface. He previously held the IWGP Tag Team Championship with longtime ally Juice Robinson, together known as FinJuice.

Juice Robinson, however, previously turned heel to become a part of the Bullet Club. With the addition of David Finlay, FinJuice has officially reunited as members of the Bullet Club and could lead the historic faction in completely new direction with their skill and talent.
