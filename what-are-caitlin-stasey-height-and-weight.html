What Are Caitlin Stasey Height And Weight?

Caitlin Stasey is an Australian actress and singer who  is known for her role as Rachel Kinski in Neighbours. In this Article, our attention will be focused on Caitlin Stasey Height and Weight. 

Who is Caitlin Stasey ?

Caitlin Jean Stasey was born on 1 May 1990 in Melbourne, a suburb of Victoria, Australia. She attended Star of the Sea College.

In 2000 Caitlin Stasey was part of the Australian girls choir that travelled around the world and took part in a re-recording of Peter Allen’s “I Still Call Australia Home”, for Qantas when she was just Ten years old.

Caitlin Stasey’s main career breakthrough arrived in September 2003 when she was cast in the children’s TV series The Sleepover Club, where she played the role of Francesca ‘Frankie’ Thomas, the self-appointed leader of the club.

In 2008, Caitlin Stasey was chosen to star in the movie adaptation of the teen novel, Tomorrow, When the War Began as the lead character Ellie Linton, a teenage girl who documents her time with a group of friends fighting the invasion of Australia.
