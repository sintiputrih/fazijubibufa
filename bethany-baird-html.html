Bethany Beal - Age, Family, Bio

Christian vlogger and writer who, alongsider her sister Kristen Clark, gives life advice to young women of faith on the web series and YouTube channel Girl Defined. Together, she and her sister co-authored the book Girl Defined: God's Radical Design for Beauty, Femininity, and Identity. 

Before Fame

She was raised in Texas. She briefly worked as a model but quit the profession after she decided that it did not align with her Christian values. 

Trivia

She has written for the Lies Young Women Believe blog and served as a pro-life counselor. 

Family Life

Her maiden name is Baird. She has a second sister named Ellissa. She got engaged to Dave Beal on May 26, 2018. They tied the knot in 2019. In 2020, she and Dave welcomed a son named David. 

Associated With

She and Jeff Johnson are both popular Christian YouTubers.
