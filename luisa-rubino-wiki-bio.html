Luisa Rubino Career

Luisa developed an interest in the world of acting at an early age. At the age of 10, Rubino started her acting career by making a debut in a TV series Juro que te amo for the role of Alumna in an episode called Odio a Violeta. From 2009 to 2016, Rubino acted Iris, Belinda, and Valeria in different episodes of La rosa de Guadalupe.

In 2012, she acted in two shows called The One Who Couldn’t Love and Par ele se Eva. Later in 2015, this actress’s first major project came after she was asked to join the cast of Simply Maria.

Her performance as Amalia/Judie/Dulce in Como dice el dicho TV series is highly appreciated by fans. Then, Luisa portrayed Patrica Vargos in Caer en Tentacion TV show created by Oscar Tabernise. In 2018, this actress’s show fugitive where she played Claudia was released. Rubino became a star after she acted in Narcos: Mexico (TV Series).
