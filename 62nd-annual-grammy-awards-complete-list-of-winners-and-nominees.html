The Complete List of 62nd Annual Grammy Awards Winner and Nominees

Check out your favorites...

One of the most awaited Awards show has finally kicked off on January 26, 2020, at the Staples Center in Los Angeles in a very thriftless way.

2019 year was one of the most dramatic and refined years for all the musicians and music lovers as well. With lots of new music sense and arrival of unseen artists, you must be wondering whether your favorites are one the list or not. 

What's more to this? Alicia Keys hosted the 62nd Annual Grammy Award ceremony, which was the beautiful show where lots of moments captured to remember. Let's take a tour of the complete list of winners and nominees of the show. 

Grammys: Record of the Year

  • Song: Bad Guy
  • Artist: Billie Eilish
  • Nominees: Hey, Ma, 7 rings, Hard Place, Talk, Old Town Road, Truth Hurts, Sunflower

  • Album: When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?

  • Nominees: I,I, Norman F***ing Rockwell!, thank u, next, I Used To Know Her, 7, Cuz I Love You(Deluxe), Father Of The Bride
