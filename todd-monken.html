Biography

Todd Monken was born in Illinois on February 5, 1966. He began coaching football in 1989 as an assistant at Grand Valley State, and made his head coaching debut in 2013 for Southern Miss. From 2011 to 2012 he was the offensive coordinator for an Oklahoma State University team coached by Mike Gundy. He played quarterback at Knox College from 1985 to 1988, where he was a small college All-American.
