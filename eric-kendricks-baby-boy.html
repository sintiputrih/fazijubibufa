Meet Minnesota Vikings Eric Kendricks Baby Boy with Wife Ally Courtnall

Eric Kendricks, born Eric-Nathan Marvin Kendricks, is one of the renowned names in the football scene. Playing as a linebacker for the NFL team, the Los Angeles Chargers, his playing career is going strong.

Talking about his personal life, the footballer recently experienced the joy of fatherhood. So, let’s dive in to know about Eric Kendricks’ baby boy and their family life.

Eric Kendricks’ Wife, With Whom He Welcomed His Son

  • The NFL star is happily married to Ally Courtnall.
  • After dating for several years, the lovebirds tied the knot on July 2, 2022.
  • Eric Kendricks’ wife, Ally Courtnall, is a model represented by Natural Model Management and Women 360 Management New York.
  • Ally, who also goes by the name Ally Kendricks, played in the defender position for the University of Southern California soccer team.
  • She is also a four-year varsity letterwinner in soccer and track for Oaks Christian.

Love Story of Eric and Ally

Eric and Ally’s love story is no less than a fairy tale. The couple knew one another for over a decade.

The duo first met in 2010 at their alma mater, UCLA, where they were student-athletes. Eric played football in the linebacker position while she played women’s soccer and track.

The duo’s first encounter occurred at a mutual friend’s birthday party. Then, they started their romantic journey together in 2013.

Over the years, the lovely pair continued enjoying their relationship and remained by one another’s side no matter what.

The lovebirds moved in together in 2015, and six years later, Ally Courtnall’s husband got down on one knee on January 30, 2021.

Then, on July 2, 2022, the engaged duo decided on happily ever after. Their friends and families graced the wedding ceremony.

Since then, the adorable duo has been enjoying their marital bliss and building a life together. And now, the lovely couple has grown their family from two to three.

Announced Pregnancy with an Emotional Video

In January 2023, the Kendricks couple shared their pregnancy news with an emotional video. Then, the duo announced to their Instagram followers with an adorable Instagram reel.

A month after their pregnancy reveal, the Kendricks couple revealed the gender of their baby with a reel video.

Ally Courtnall and Eric Kendricks’ baby boy is fortunate to grow up in a loving household. Well, it will be too soon to talk about his career plans.

We hope Knight grows up to be a fine man, and we look forward to knowing what the Kendricks family will be doing in the coming days.
