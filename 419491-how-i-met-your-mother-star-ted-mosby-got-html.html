The actor took to Instagram to share this delightful news with a series of snapshots from his special day, accompanied by the caption, "I got married! Two weeks ago. In a light blizzard."

The wedding was truly a winter wonderland, as Radnor described it on Instagram as "an incredible overwhelming snowy bliss-filled weekend." It was a testament to their love story, with 164 guests braving the biting 22-degree air to witness the union.
