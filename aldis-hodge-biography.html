Aldis Hodge Bio (Wiki)

Who is Aldis Hodge?

Aldis Hodge is an American actor best known for his appearances as MC Ren in the 2015 biopic Straight Outta Compton and as Alec Hardison on TNT’s Leverage.

Apart from that, he wrapped production on “City on a Hill,” a Showtime pilot produced by Matt Damon and Ben Affleck in which he co-starred alongside Kevin Bacon.
