A$AP Rocky Deletes His Instagram Strengthening New Music Rumors

A$AP Rocky has been hinting at a return to music since dropping a song through the infamous Cyberpunk 2077 video game last December.

Since then, Flacko has been teasing fans with snippets of music and clips of him rapping with the Mob and filming a music video. He even dropped a feature on British rapper Slowthai’s new single “MAZZA” on Friday (January 8). Now, the Harlem-bred rapper has made yet another hint at dropping new music, this time on his Instagram page.

On Monday (January 11), Rocky cleared his entire account leaving only the words GRIM, LORDPRETTYFLVCKOJODYE11 and the link to his creative company AWGE in the bio.

Whenever an artist decides to wipe out all his posts on the popular photosharing app, it can sometimes mean something big is on the horizon. Meek Mill, The Weeknd and Future did it when they dropped their respective albums in 2017.

The Freebandz leader actually did it again last week when he got rid of all the posts on his page after teasing fans with pictures and Instagram Stories of him in the studio with DJ Esco, Metro Boomin and Southside.

Whatever Rocky’s reason may be for clearing out his page, fans are excited to see what he and the Mob have been cooking up. On January 4, the brothers were seen together for the first time since A$AP Ferg was allegedly kicked out of the group by one of its founders A$AP Illz in September.
