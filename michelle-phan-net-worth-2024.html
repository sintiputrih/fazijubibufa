Early Life and Career Beginnings

Michelle Phan’s journey to success began with her passion for art and makeup. Born to Vietnamese immigrants, Phan’s early life was not without its challenges. However, her love for makeup artistry and her desire to share her skills with others led her to post her first makeup tutorial on YouTube in 2007. This marked the beginning of what would become a highly influential career in the digital space.

The YouTube Phenomenon

Phan’s YouTube channel quickly gained popularity, making her one of the first beauty influencers to achieve mainstream recognition. Her detailed tutorials and genuine personality resonated with millions of viewers. As her subscriber count grew, so did her opportunities, including partnerships with major beauty brands and the launch of her own makeup line.

Em Cosmetics and Other Ventures

In 2013, Phan launched her own makeup line, Em Cosmetics, in partnership with L’Oréal. Although the initial launch faced challenges, Phan later acquired the company and relaunched it with a new vision. This move showcased her resilience and business acumen, contributing significantly to her net worth.

Expanding the Empire: ipsy and Thematic

Phan’s entrepreneurial spirit didn’t stop with Em Cosmetics. She co-founded ipsy, a beauty subscription service that capitalized on the personalized beauty trend. Additionally, she founded Thematic, a music licensing platform that connects content creators with musicians. These ventures have played a crucial role in building her fortune.
