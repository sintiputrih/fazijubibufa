Kathy Griffin, 53, Does ALS Ice Bucket Challenge Naked: Video

Just when you thought the stars had run out of creative ways to do the ALS Ice Bucket Challenge, along comes Kathy Griffin! The 53-year-old comedian appeared on Jimmy Kimmel Live on Friday, Aug. 29, to discuss her new comedy album and to air her raunchy challenge video. 

In the clip, the redheaded star slips out of a pool — entirely nude — while wearing sky-high red stiletto heels (“I’m classy lady!”). Griffin accepted the unusual take on the viral challenge from author and financial advisor Suze Orman, and some of her other challengers, Backstreet Boys member A.J. McLean and Parks and Recreation star Aubrey Plaza, were the ones to dump the freezing water on her head. 

She then proceeded to shove McLean into the pool as Plaza flipped off the camera saying, “F–k you, ALS!”
