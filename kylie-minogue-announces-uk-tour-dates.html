Kylie Minogue Announces UK Tour Dates

Kylie Minogue has announced her UK tour dates to take place early next summer.

Kylie Minogue's single "I Can't Get You Out Of My Head" has been number one in the UK for four weeks and the album has topped the charts.

So far the following dates have been booked:

– 29/4: International Arena, Cardiff.
– 7/5: N.E.C., Birmingham
– 11/5: M.E.N. Arena, Manchester
– 14/5: Sheffield Arena, Sheffield
– 17/5: S.E.C.C., Glasgow
– 21/5: Telewest Arena, Newcastle
– 24/5: Wembley Arena, London
