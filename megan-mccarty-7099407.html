Megan McCarty - InStyle

Title: Writer

Education: University of Minnesota

Location: Los Angeles, California

Megan McCarty is a full-time freelance writer, editor, etc. who writes about the fun stuff: design, commerce, travel, wellness, beauty, and fashion. With over 15 years of experience, she prides herself on finding the best of the best products, sources, and getaways within those categories.

Highlights:

  • Over 15 years of experience in the field.
  • Received a B.A. from the University of Minnesota.

Experience

Megan's outlets include Domino, InStyle, People, Architectural Digest, USA Today’s Reviewed, MyDomaine, Bustle, Well+Good, The Spruce, House & Home, Forbes, Hunker, and more. Her life rules include, but are not limited to: contribute to your IRA, zipper when merging, and always do the nice thing.

Education

Megan has a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Minnesota, where she majored in Magazine Journalism as well as Cultural Studies and Comparative Literature.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
