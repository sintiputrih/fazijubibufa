Suge Knight Net Worth


Suge Knight’s net worth is estimated to be $200,000. Marion Hugh Knight Jr., commonly known as Suge Knight, is a famous American executive producer, bodyguard, and promoter. He is perhaps best known as a co-founder and the CEO of the record company called “Death Row Record” which reached international success with the release of Dr. Dre’s debut studio album “The Chronic” in 1991.

In 2007, he earned $4.56 million from the sale of his house in Malibu and in 2009, he accumulated $5,000 after selling his jackets, and $2,500 for the sale of “Death Row Records” electric chair.

Suge Knight was born in 1965, in California, United States, where he studied at the Lynwood High School and later attended the University of Nevada. Knight’s professional football career wasn’t successful, so he decided to pursue a career in music, founding the “Death Row Records” label. It was with “Death Row Records” that Knight became a well-known face in the industry, especially after signing artists like Tupac Shakur and Snoop Dogg. However, he faced legal troubles including accusations of contributing to Tupac’s murder. As of April 2015, Suge has been charged with murder and attempted murder over a hit-and-run, following an altercation on the set of a commercial for the film “Straight Outta Compton.”

Suge Knight’s net worth is estimated to be $200,000, which he has gathered due to his involvement in the music industry.
