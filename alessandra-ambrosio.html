Alessandra Ambrosio News - Us Weekly

Name:Alessandra Ambrosio

Hometown:Erechim, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil

Birthday:April 11, 1981

About

Alessandra Ambrosio is one of the world’s highest-paid models. Ambrosio began her modeling career at age 12, going on to compete in Brazil’s Elite Model Look competition and land the cover of Brazilian Elle. She later booked jobs with major brands and fashion houses like Guess, Gucci, Calvin Klein and Oscar de la Renta, and she was a Victoria’s Secret Angel from 2004 to 2017.

Ambrosio was engaged to businessman Jamie Mazur from 2008 to 2018. The exes share daughter Anja (born in 2008) and son Noah (born in 2012). Alessandra was first linked to boyfriend Richard Lee in May 2021.
